Skills



Air Brush Makeup

I am an expert in airbrush makeup. I have been using the technique for years and have honed my skills to create flawless, long-lasting looks for my clients. I enjoy the precision and control that airbrush makeup allows me to have, and I can create a wide range of effects, from natural and subtle to dramatic and bold. I am constantly learning new techniques and staying up-to-date with the latest products and trends in the industry to ensure that my clients always look their best. I am passionate about helping my clients feel confident and beautiful, and airbrush makeup is the perfect tool to achieve that goal.





HD Makeup

I am a skilled professional in HD makeup. With the ability to provide high-definition looks and flawless finish, I have the knowledge of how to work with various camera and lighting setups. I have a great understanding of how makeup appears on camera, and I use this knowledge to enhance my client's features, ensuring they look their best on screen. I am skilled in using makeup products that provide a natural look, while still being able to conceal imperfections. I am always up-to-date with the latest trends and products in the industry, and I am passionate about helping my clients look their best, whether they're on a film set or on a video call.





Hairstyling

I am a highly skilled hair stylist, with a passion for creating beautiful, unique hairstyles for my clients. I am well-versed in a wide range of cutting, coloring, and styling techniques, and I am always staying up-to-date with the latest trends and innovations in the industry. I am able to work with all hair types, textures, and lengths, and I am adept at creating looks that are both classic and contemporary. I have a keen eye for detail, and I take great care to ensure that every cut, color and style is tailored to my client's individual needs and preferences. I believe that every person's hair is unique, and I strive to bring out the best in each client's hair, whether they are looking for a dramatic change or a subtle update.
